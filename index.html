The Humanity collection is about emotions, about fighting your fears and yourself. About how people submit to their emotions and suppress them.

Shifting our beliefs about emotions.

This brings us to the pivotal questions:

01.

Can we alter our perspectives on emotions?

02.

What shapes our emotions?

03.

Are they a product of past experiences and personal history, or do hormonal shifts and changes in brain chemistry dictate our feelings?

04.

Is it possible to induce emotions we'd rather avoid? Ultimately, why do we require emotions?

The Humanity collection is an exploration into the world of human emotions through the prism of visual art.
